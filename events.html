Our Events

We have successfully hosted high-profile street football tournaments and brand activations across Egypt,
Engaging players and spectators in dynamic experiences.

Elfit Activation November 2024

El-7areefa Game took its unique style of football to Elfit, engaging over 500+ fitness enthusiasts in a two-day activation. This event not only introduced panna and street football to a new audience but also expanded our reach, forging connections with both sports and fitness communities.

⚽

Cairo October 2024

Cairo’s tournament set the stage for an electrifying showdown. 24 participants battled for supremacy in a night filled with skillful plays and intense rivalries. The high-energy event captivated an audience of 300+ fans, reinforcing El-7areefa Game’s reputation as the leading street football platform in Egypt.

⚽

Suez July 2024

This tournament not only showcased elite talent but also became a viral sensation. A storytelling video of the winner (Hossam) from the tournament hit 1 million views on Facebook, sparking 500+ inquiries from aspiring players eager to participate in future competitions.

⚽

Alexandria April 2024

Our Alexandria event was a true celebration of skill and passion. Featuring 48 players competing in both 1v1 Panna and 3v3 Street Football tournaments, the event created an electric atmosphere, drawing in over 500 enthusiastic spectators. The matches were intense, with incredible displays of technique and creativity that left the crowd in awe.
